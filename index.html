A LITTLE BIT ABOUT MYSELF

I was born and raised just outside of Salt Lake City, Ut. Being raised in Utah has instilled in me, a heathy passion for the outdoors and adventure of all kinds.

I pride myself on being a maker of all things. I have an obsession with adding as many tools to my 'box of trick' as I can. A few of my passions are:

  • Graphic Design
  • Web Design
  • Photography
  • Recording & Performing Music
  • Screen Printing
  • Wood Working
  • Product Development & Design

I currently work as a Graphic/Front End Web Developer in the motorsports industry. I have over 16 years of experience in the graphic design field. Starting off self-taught, then refining my skills at the Art Institute, has given me a great overview of graphic design. With that said, I am currently learning new things every day.

I would like to make the switch to programming and creating web/app infrustrctures. With this knowledge and what I know about graphic design, I feel I could achieve or contribute to great works.
